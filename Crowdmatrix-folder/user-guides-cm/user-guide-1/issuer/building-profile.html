How to Create an Awesome Company Profile

Before you start

Write for Investors

Make sure all your content is geared towards investors and not customers! Frame everything your write around what investors want to know, and what's valuable from an investment perspective.

UTILIZE & TWEAK EXISTING ASSETS

Collect all your business documents - pitch decks, marketing copy etc. The important thing is you make the neccesary changes to gear them towards investors and your raise on Crowdmatrix.

ENGAGE & CONVERT

It's your responsibility to create a deal profile that engages and excites investors. You need to ensure that you are converting visitors to your deal page into investors in your company.

Highlights Section

Give a brief, capturing introduction of your company. This is an important section since the text will be the first thing an investor reads on your company profile and your overview will be used in your company tombstone and in emails that are sent out to investors. Keep it short, 2-3 sentences.

The rest of the section headers are customizable. You should think critically about what are the most important points about your company and your deal that you want investors to know about and then use the 3 sections to highlight those points.

Tips

People read the content at the TOP of the page most thoroughly…so make sure this section is captivating and clear.

Product Section

  • Talk about the technology behind your product and why it should excite investors
  • What makes your product unique and why is it competetive
  • The ‘Magic’ of your product and what makes it s great investment

Tips

This section it optional and should only be used if you company revolves around a product.

Gallery Section

The gallery section is where you should put important images and videos that explain your company or product to investors.

Images to include:

  • Flowcharts
  • Diagrams
  • Prototypes
  • Screenshots

Types of videos to include:

  • Product demos
  • ‘A message from the Founders’ to investors
  • Company promotional video

Tips

Overlay text, and use circles/arrows to bring focus to the specific area of the screenshot you want investors to look at. Don’t make investors think about what you're trying to show them in a screenshot, make it easy for them to see it.

Custom Section

Custom sections are very important to utilize, since each company is very different, adding custom sections allow you to tailor your deal page to your own unique needs. We recommend using at least 2-3 custom sections.

Suggestions:

  • Market Opportunity
  • Monetization Strategy
  • Market Growth
  • Roadmap for Your Future

Combine Mutiple Custom Sections

You can create ONE large ‘custom section’ with multiple pictures and content by creating multiple ‘custom sections' with only the TITLE inputted into the first custom section.

Tips

You can move around the order of your custom section by using the arrows to the right of the titles.You can also insert a slideshare of a presentation

You can add a feature image, video or slideshare to the top of each custom section

Popular Questions

Add frequently asked questions with answers- but keep in mind they should be geared towards investor questions, and not customer or user questions.

Suggestions:

  • Who are your competitors and how are you different?
  • What features/parts of your product do your customers love the most?
  • How do you acquire new customers?
  • How is your product evolving?
  • What business objectives do you accomplish with the money you raise?

Tips

What questions do investors usually ask after you pitch them? This is the spot to get ahead of those questions and provide answers before investors ask.
